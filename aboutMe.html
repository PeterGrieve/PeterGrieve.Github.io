About Me:

I was born in 1995 in the city of Waukesha, Wisconsin. I lived in Wisconsin until the age 10. In the summer of 2005, my parents and I moved to Dover, New Hampshire. I have lived in Dover ever since, eventually attending Dover High school. after graduating from Dover High, I started studying programming and computer science at NHTI, Concord's Community College under the Animation and Game Graphic Programming major. I graduated from NHTI in spring of 2017 with an Associate's degree. After graduating from NHTI, I took a wander year to travel. I visited the west coast, Canada, the United Kingdom, and France. Having completed my wander year, I have spent the last six months attending a "Coding Bootcamp" hosted by UNH. Under the Bootcamp, I have learned many new programming languages and technologies. My next ambition is to become a full-time junior developer.
